## Reservation Policy

Reservations are recommended for dinner.

Same-day reservations are allowed if tables are available.

Large groups of 7 or more guests require manual confirmation.

Standard reservation duration is 120 minutes.

Tables are held for 15 minutes after the reservation time.

## Cancellation Policy

Guests can cancel free of charge until 2 hours before the reservation.

Late cancellations may be noted by the restaurant team.

## Menu Highlights

Popular dishes include hummus, grilled halloumi, lamb skewers, mezze platters, falafel, and baklava.

Vegetarian options are available.

Vegan options are available on request.

Gluten-free options are limited and should be confirmed with the restaurant.
